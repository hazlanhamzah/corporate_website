Feedback FAQs

How long does it take to get a response to my feedback?

We typically respond within 3-5 working days. For urgent matters, please visit the library counter.

Can I submit feedback anonymously?

Yes, you can leave out your personal details, but this means we won't be able to respond directly to your feedback.

What happens after I submit feedback?

All feedback is reviewed by our library team. We may contact you for more details and will consider all suggestions for improvements.
