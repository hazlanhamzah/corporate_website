Online Renewal

Renew your borrowed items online without visiting the library.

How to Renew Online

  1. Visit the Online Catalog and click "My Account"
  2. Login with your student ID and password (default: IC number)
  3. Select the items you wish to renew
  4. Click "Renew Selected Items"
  5. Note your new due dates

Items cannot be renewed if: they are overdue, another user has placed a hold, or you've reached your renewal limit.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a library account?

All registered students automatically have a library account. Your login is your student ID number, and the default password is your IC number (without hyphens). Teachers and staff should see the librarian to set up their accounts.

What are the library opening hours?

The library is open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 4:30 PM. We are closed on weekends and public holidays. During school holidays, we operate on reduced hours - please check our News page for updates.

What happens if I return a book late?

Late returns incur fines of RM0.20 per day per item for students, and RM0.50 per day per item for teachers/staff. If an item is more than 30 days overdue, your borrowing privileges will be suspended until the item is returned or replaced.

Can I reserve a book that's checked out?

Yes, you can place a hold on checked out items through the Online Catalog. You'll be notified when the item is available, and it will be held for you at the circulation desk for 3 days.

Does the library have e-books?

Yes! We have a growing collection of e-books available through our Digital Resources page. You can access them anytime with your library account.
